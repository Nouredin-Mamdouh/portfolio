About Me

Assalamu Alaikum! I'm Nouredin Mamdouh Mohamed Eldesouky Elnamla, a Year 2 student pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Science in Software Engineering at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM).

I'm passionate about web development because I enjoy bringing ideas to life in the browser. The ability to design, code, and see instant results fuels my interest in creating dynamic and user-friendly web experiences.

Currently, I'm sharpening my skills in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and PHP as part of my SECV2223 Web Programming coursework. I’ve developed several academic projects, including a responsive personal website and a simple CRUD-based task manager using front-end and back-end integration.

Outside of tech, I spend time learning more about Islam and enjoy editing videos. I believe that creativity, whether in code or content, helps build better digital experiences — and I strive to grow in both areas.
